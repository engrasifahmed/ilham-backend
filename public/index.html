Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Ilham Education Consultancy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our consultancy services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, academic background, and financial documents necessary for university applications and visa processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process university applications and scholarship requests.
  • To provide visa guidance and travel support.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application status.
  • To send newsletters or updates (if subscribed).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share it with:

  • Partner universities for admission purposes.
  • Visa authorities (embassies) as required for processing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your documents and personal information from unauthorized access.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at info@ilhameducation.com.
